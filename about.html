JoJo's Biography

JoJo grew up in Milwaukee, WI and received his B.S. in biology from UW-Madison. Upon graduation, he researched algal genomics and morphology at the New York Botanical Garden, worked at the Molecular Genomics Core at the University of Southern California, and analyzed the skin microbiome in the Kalan Lab and UW-Madison.

Currently, JoJo works in the Currie Lab as a Genetics PhD student, and is interested in the mechanisms of evolutionary innovation in the fungus-farming ants. >>Learn more about JoJo's research interests.

Outside of lab, JoJo enjoys basketball and music.
